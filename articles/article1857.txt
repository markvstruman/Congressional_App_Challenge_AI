The regime in Tehran has conducted a second public execution of someone, this time a twenty three year-old man, involved in the nationwide anti-government protests. The Iranian government says the man fatally stabbed two members of the Basij paramilitary force but human rights groups say he faced a sham trial. FOX’s Alex Hogan speaks with Cameron Khansarinia, Policy Director at the ‘National Union for Democracy In Iran’, about these violent tactics and whether or not they will discourage protesters.
